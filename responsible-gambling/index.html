Set Limits Before Play

Limits work best when they are set before opening a game, live table or sports market. A clear budget makes decisions easier when the session becomes fast or emotional.

  • Choose a fixed amount for the session.
  • Set a time limit and take breaks.
  • Do not increase stakes to recover losses.
  • Keep gambling separate from essential money.

Warning Signs

Warning signs include chasing losses, hiding gambling activity, borrowing money to play, losing track of time, feeling unable to stop or becoming stressed about account results.

If any of these signs appear, stop playing and consider using account controls, taking a break or seeking support from a responsible-gambling organisation.

Breaks and Support

A break can be helpful when gambling starts to feel automatic or stressful. Users may also contact support about account controls where available, including limits or temporary restrictions.

Support should be used early. Waiting until the situation becomes severe makes it harder to regain control.
